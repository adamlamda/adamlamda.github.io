Smith's Bible Dictionary
Iru

(watch), the eldest son of the great Caleb son of Jephunneh. (1 Chronicles 4:15) (B.C. 1451.)

International Standard Bible Encyclopedia
IRU

i'-roo (`iru): Eldest son of Caleb (1 Chronicles 4:15); probably to be read Ir, the syllable "-u" being the conjunction "and" belonging to the following word.
