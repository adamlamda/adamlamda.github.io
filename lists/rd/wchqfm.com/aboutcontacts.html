Crescent Hill Radio, Inc,  DBA - WCHQ,  is a nonprofit corporation
​with 501(c)3 tax exempt status.
Everyone at WCHQ is a volunteer. Including station manager, engineers and on air personalities. ~Thanks for Listening!


WCHQ FM is an FCC licensed Low Power FM Station, broadcasting 24/7 at 100.9 FM and streaming on the web.  
​The internet stream can played from this website by using the player above on the left, or on devices using 
The WCHQ Mobile App (free) or TuneIn Radio and other Radio apps for iPhones or Androids.
Our goal is to provide local bands and musicians a place to air their music, and provide a community radio station
with participation from community organizations and local businesses.  

“The mission of WCHQ LP is to connect the community with local and regional music and programming,
​using a fair and non-competitive format.”​

We reach out to local artists, organizations and individuals who would like an outlet for their recordings, performances, or announcement of their events and shows. We also feature weekly talk shows about local topics and have live DJ's, who not only play all local music, but announce community events and help promote charitable organizations. Our database of local music, from Louisville, all of Kentucky and Southern Indiana contains over 32,000 songs.
If you have a community announcement for our blog, which our DJs read from on air,  please send an email to Info@WCHQfm.com.  
If you would like to show your support of the community and WCHQ,
Business Underwriting Opportunities are available, as we are a 501(c)3 organization.
We are fully licensed with ASCAP, BMI, SESAC and SoundExchange  
​If you are a band or singer/songwriter and have your own CD or digital music, we would love to give you some air time.
