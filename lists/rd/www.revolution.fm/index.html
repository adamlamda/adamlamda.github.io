MAYHEM 2018

Revolution 91.7’s 15th annual Mayhem concert is in the books. Thanks to everyone who attended the show and thanks to J Roddy Walston & The Business, The Josephines, The Rift, Kenzie Crowe and the Flaming Hots, Internet Boyfriend and Solar Disco Force for their stellar performances.

Also our gratitude to Rapid Rick and The Status Crowe for being on hand to sell food and drinks and Musician’s Pro for their outstanding stage, sound and lighting work.

 

Revolution is more than just a student radio station. Since when did learning platforms sound this good?

Rev is southern Kentucky’s premier source for the latest alternative hits. Jam out to the tunes of simpler times every weekday with Nostalgia at Noon, or rock out to  Rev After Hours from eight in the evening to three in the morning.

Got a song you want to hear on air? Give us a call or text at the studio at 270-681-7917.
