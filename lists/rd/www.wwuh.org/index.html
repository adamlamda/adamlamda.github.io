WWUH Celebrates 50th Anniversary Year

On July 15, 1968, WWUH went on the air for the first time as the voice of the University of Hartford. From day one, the station was committed to providing the greater Hartford area with professionally-produced alternative programming not available on commercial stations. WWUH will be hosting a number of on- and off-air events in celebration of our 50th Anniversary. At 4:30 p.m. on Sunday, July 15 we aired a special 30 minute documentary about our early years produced by volunteer Brandon Kampe. To learn more about the station's history, click here.
