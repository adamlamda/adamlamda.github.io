  • Quality television

    WGTE provides national and local programming for all ages - local content such as Toledo Stories; quality children's programming such as Sesame Street and popular dramas such as Masterpiece Theater. FIND OUT MORE

  • Radio that Engages

    FM 91 radio delivers national news from NPR and local programs such as The Rough Draft Diaries with Haley Taylor, Life as We Know It with Tom Walton and New Music from Bowling Green.  Listeners can enjoy a varied broadcast including news, classical jazz, folk and eclectic music. LISTEN NOW

  • Educational Resources

    WGTE's Early Learning Department and Educational Resource Center helps adults encourage young children in their love of learning and provides schools and teachers educational materials which embrace 21st century learning skills and enhance learning. LEARN MORE

  • About WGTE Public Media

    WGTE is a center of learning and education. We were founded as an educational institution, and our educational mission remains at the heart of what we do every day. From our entertaining, informative and educational programs that air on WGTE TV and FM 91, to our professional development opportunities for K-12 teachers and preschool day care providers, WGTE helps to make northwest Ohio and southeast Michigan a better place to live.
