Public Affairs Show

WMMR is committed to providing meaningful programming addressing the community’s needs and concerns. Community leaders and organization spokespersons are encouraged to contact us to request an appearance on The Public Affairs Show, a weekly 30 minute interview program addressing the needs and concerns of our area. Topics may include health, literacy, public safety, education, charitable initiatives, politics and government, marriage, children, the arts, societal issues, technology, drug & alcohol abuse, and others.
