About us

Since 1981, WGMU Radio has been the student-run radio station of George Mason University. WGMU streams 24/7, providing a diverse collection of both popular and more underground music. Currently we have 80 hours of live and original programming every week, covering everything from sports and news to talk shows and the hottest music. You can listen to WGMU on iTunes Radio, Radioflag.com, our website's webcam feed and on any smartphone via our MPEG stream on the top of this page.
