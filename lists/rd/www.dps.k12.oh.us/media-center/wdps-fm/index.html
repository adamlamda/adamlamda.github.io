WDPS FM

 

 

 

 

 

 

WDPS FM is Dayton's only jazz radio station. We are a non-commercial radio station; however, we do accept underwriting.

We broadcast from the new David H. Ponitz Career Technology Center in downtown Dayton. Our station is staffed by students and volunteers.

Listen Live Monday - Friday from 9:15am to 4:30pm for today's best jazz!
