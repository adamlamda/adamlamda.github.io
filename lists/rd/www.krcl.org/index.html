Get RadioActive - Weeknights at 6 p.m.

Monday, Aug. 27: Never Again is Now, with human rights lawyer Erika George and Rick Okabe, board member of the Topaz Japanese American Internment Camp Museum. Poet Rob Carney, writing for change. Plus, March For Our Lives SLC is back to host the show.

Tuesday, Aug. 28: Utah’s new Hispanic Workforce Safety Alliance, an alliance of organizations and institutions for the ethical protection and preservation of the lives and welfare of workers. Plus, updates from Where are the Women?, a People’s Hearing on the San Rafael Swell, and the Community Coalition for Inland Port Reform. Hosts: Lara Jones and Ma Black.

Wednesday, Aug. 29: Utah’s real health crisis: overdose deaths. The Addict’s Mom and Overdose Awareness Utah return to RadioActive in advance of Overdose Awareness Day. Hosts: Billy Palmer and Nick Burns.

Thursday, Aug. 30: Skateboard Diplomacy: What You Can Do For Your Country with Neftalie Williams, Spocks Skate Camp and the Utah Council for Citizen Diplomacy. Plus, a checkup of the KRCL studios with GM Tristin Tabish. Hosts: Lara Jones and Billy Palmer.

Friday, Aug. 31: Punk Rock Farmer Friday, featuring the Urban Farm Report with Rose Park Crop Swap, and live music from Emby Alexander. Hosts: Lara Jones and Aldine Strychnine, KRCL’s Punk Rock Farmer.

Got a story you'd like to share on the show? Record a voice memo on your smart phone, then email it to radioactive@krcl.org. Keep it under 3 minutes and be sure to include a name and phone number in the body of the message.
