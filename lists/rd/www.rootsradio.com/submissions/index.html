Roots Radio welcomes submissions of all kinds. Here you will find information on how to submit music for airplay consideration, news tips, calendar events, promotional requests, and general comments. To inquire about underwriting on Roots Radio, please click here. 

 
 

MUSIC FOR AIRPLAY CONSIDERATION

We're building our library on an ongoing basis. We accept both digital and physical music submissions. If submitting a digital copy, please include the name of the group and the project name. Please include BOTH a link to a Soundcloud or other easy-preview method, AS WELL as a link to a downloadable .WAV file (no .mp3s accepted.)
