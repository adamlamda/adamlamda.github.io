It is the primary mission of Lasell College Radio to serve as the ultimate Free College Radio station and Connected Learning experience at Lasell. WLAS offers college students numerous radio positions with real-world experience outside of the classroom. In addition to free college radio, we also offer free podcasts of live band performances, interviews, and more!

About our College Radio Station and Free Podcasts

Our college radio and podcasts aim to promote the hottest new acts and developing artists. Our unique contribution to the music industry creates fresh, original podcasts and free college radio that sets Lasell College apart from commercial radio and mainstream play.

More than just Free College Radio and Podcasts

Lasell College Radio is a resource for our community to be heard, on 102.9FM, online, on mobile, and streaming. Join us at our college radio station or work with us through public service announcements, podcasts, promotions & other radio collaboratives!
