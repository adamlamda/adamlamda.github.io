Unmasking Domestic Violence Conference – Oct. 24-26

Domestic violence is one of the leading causes of death for women here in south carolina. The M.A.D. team, Men Against Domestic Violence, work tirelessly to push this conversation forward and want to invite you to their first annual domestic violence conference, Oct. 24 through Oct. 26 at the College of Charleston North Campus.
