Welcome

Welcome to Gospel 9 Radio

"We're Your Christian Voice"

WYCV has been a full time Southern Gospel Station since 1983, and operates at 900Khz (900AM) with a clear channel. We currently broadcast 24hrs. a day 7 days a week. 
We’re a commercial station reaching a population of over 600,000 people, transmitting at 2500 watts.
WYCV – Gospel 9 Radio – is owned and operated by Freedom Broadcasting
Corporation.  President and General Manager, Dr. Marvin “Buddy” Sizemore. 
The studio is located at 398 South Main Street (US Highway 321-A) Granite Falls, NC,  less than 2 miles from the city limits and 3 miles from Hickory, NC.

Our goal is to reach the lost for Christ and encourage believers through great sermons and Southern Gospel Music.

Let us know what you think. Our listeners are the back-bone of WYCV Gospel 9.

We look forward to hearing from you.
