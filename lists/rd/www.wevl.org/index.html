A Message From WEVL's Board of Directors

Details
Written by WEVL FM 89.9
Published: Friday, 24 August 2018

We, the WEVL Board of Directors, would like to take this opportunity to address some recently raised questions regarding the operation and future of the station. WEVL's Board of Directors charged a Development Exploratory Committee to explore growth options and present them to the Board for approval and subsequent action. We agree with many of the suggestions and are already actively pursuing several, including the development of an actionable strategic plan, improving station facilities, and increasing diverse voices and programming. 

After reviewing and approving initial recommendations, WEVL's Board felt it necessary to dissolve the Development Exploratory Committee–-there was concern that possible efforts and projects discussed by the Committee with third parties could be mistaken for efforts already approved by the Board. This decision was made because the pace of activity was accelerating beyond what was responsibly manageable and outpacing thorough vetting and discussion.

In short, the dissolution of the Committee doesn't mean the Board doesn't share the goals or visions being discussed; the Board simply decided it needed to take a direct role, rather than a supervisory one.

Ultimately, we believe all involved have WEVL's best interests for the future at the forefront, and we look forward to working together. There are as many different opinions on what WEVL is and should be as there are listeners; the station is a result of a balanced combination of those perspectives. We firmly believe WEVL has a bright future as an unique and valuable asset to Memphis, the Mid-South and beyond

Tim Taylor, President / Steffen Schreiner, Vice President / Hal Mabray, Secretary / Joyce Cobb / Brian Craig / Kelly Kraisinger

Donate Your Vehicle and Help WEVL

Details

Published: Wednesday, 10 August 2016
