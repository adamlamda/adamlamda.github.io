Sheep happens.
Black Sheep Radio has swag! Swell shirts, sweet hats, darling bumper stickers! All proceeds help keep WOOL.fm, your volunteer run, non-profit, noncommercial community radio station on the air.
An active participant in the Emergency Alert System, WOOL.fm keeps you informed during local, state, and national emergencies. We've got your back. We'll let you know if something's up.
WOOL.fm is an affiliate of the Pacifica Network, a listener sponsored alternative to commercially driven media. Much of the syndicated content we run originates from other Pacifica affiliates.
As a member, you can have your own weekly noncommercial radio program! Be a DJ, be a talk show host! Our mission to entertain, inform, and elevate the community can be a whole lot of fun.
Have your community-minded event announced on the air! It's a free public service that we offer. See the Mid-River Bulletin Board.
Underwriting is a good outreach solution for your business, organization, cultural event, or occasion! You can reach our listeners 24/7 on the air and on our website, and support WOOL.fm at the same time.
Donations are tax-deductible. It costs more money than you might think to keep this community resource on the air. Surprisingly, we can't get by on just our good looks and delightful personality.
What's on now? Take a look at our programming schedule where you'll find everything from nuanced talk about political and social issues, to just-plain-fun, to our own hometown DJs.
Listen now! 91.5 FM in the area, or anywhere in the world via internet stream on your favorite media player. Listen on the go with free apps for Droid, Mac, and Windows phones, tablets, and computers!
Join us! WOOL.fm is commercial free, member owned, volunteer run, and 100% supported by the community... your friends and neighbors. Just us regular people. Together, we're mighty.
Member owned, volunteer run, nonprofit, commercial free WOOL.fm hears you: send surface mail to P.O. Box 110, Bellows Falls VT 05101; phone 802-460-WOOL; or send an email by clicking this message.
