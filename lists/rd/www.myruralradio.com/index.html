It gives us GREAT PLEASURE to introduce you to our NEW 2017 Artists of the Year! Steven Courtney and his band have been a part of MyRuralRadio.com since it's inception some 9 years ago. A two-time Fall Music Festival performer and MyRuralSundays guest, Steven is a big part of our family and our history.

Mr. Courtney has dedicated his life to music, whether it be writing, performing or producing. He has played for people of all ages over the course of his career and has a discography that would surprise many. Most importanty, to me, he is my friend. Whenever I lose my faith in people or get caught up in the negative aspects that life throws at me, I turn to Steven for advice or guidance or inspiration. He always reminds us to "Walk in love and let love hold you close".

Thank you Steven,

Michael S. Males
Station Founder

Official Website: www.stevencourtneyband.com/

In the span of 30 plus years of writing, performing and recording Steven Courtney has maintained a contest flow of roots and blues music to become a beloved public figure throughout Central Pennsylvania and beyond.

Steven Courtney Band is loaded with talent and seasoned professionals who deliver a rich blend of roadhouse blues , honky-tonk rhythms, raw, straight-ahead juke joint sound with folky mojo vibe. Bandmates include Doug Walton on electric guitar, Andy Lake on keys, Eric Foster on drums and Bobby Wehner on bass. Session player and talented sideman Andrew Jacobs sits in with the band as well.

The band hit the studio in November 2016 nine tracking tunes for their debut album titled American Road. The album is available now through CDBaby.

Join the Steven Courtney Band mailing list for periodic show updates on the Official Website: www.stevencourtneyband.com/
