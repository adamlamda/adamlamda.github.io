Watch The Listen Local Show

Listen Local since 2003

Listen Local has been proudly serving San Diego's local music community since October of 2003 producing music festivals, songwriter showcases, radio and web shows, cookbooks, calendars, music compilations, camping events and a weekly blog. Beginning in 2018 The Listen Local Show will be a monthly show hosted by the band CalAmity and broadcast from The Heritage Ranch. The show will feature movers and shakers from San Diego's creative scene and events and updates from The San Dieguito Heritage Museum.

Interested in being featured on the show? Pre recorded interviews take place at The Heritage Ranch in Encinitas. Contact Cathryn to schedule yours.

Listen Local also offers affordable video and photo services plus funky fashion via our mobile stage/studio/store, The Green Room. Let us know how we can help you!

Songwriter Sundays!

Join us at The Heritage Ranch most Sundays for an afternoon of trading songs with nice folks as we  gather under the patio for an unplugged jam circle. Seating for 10, bring a chair just in case.  All ages and abilities, small donation to Listen Local appreciated. Contact Cathryn@ListenLocalRadio.com with any questions.
