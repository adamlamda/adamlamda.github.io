NCC Press Release: WONC Celebrates 50 years!

May 10, 2018 / WONC-FM / Comments Off on NCC Press Release: WONC Celebrates 50 years!

The award-winning, student run radio station WONC-FM 89.1 celebrates its 50th anniversary on May 11, 2018.

The celebration will kick off at 10 a.m. both inside and out on the lawn of Oliver Hall, 232 E. Chicago Ave., the station’s home since it moved from Old Main in 1985. The event’s master of ceremony will be James Godo ‘93, North Central’s assistant vice president for external affairs, special assistant to the president and alumnus of the station.
