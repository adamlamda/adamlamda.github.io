Submissions

How to Submit Your Music to NWCZ Radio.
  1. Attach your .mp3 file with metadata to include “SONG TITLE”, “ALBUM TITLE” and “ARTIST NAME” and email to NWCZRadio@gmail.com
How To ADD Metadata.
  1. To ADD metadat using Windows os – Right click on file and select “properties”.  Then click the “details” tab and fill in info.

 

2.  To ADD metadata using “i-tunes (windows or mac)” – open the file in iTunes, right click on it and click “get info.” Click the “info” tab then fill in as much as possible, and send it our way!
