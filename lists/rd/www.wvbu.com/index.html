This is VBU!

WVBU is The Voice of Bucknell University! We are the only student-run radio station on the Bucknell University campus.

Tune in to WVBU for a diverse offering of music and programming. From our carefully-curated music lineup, to student shows, to full-season coverage of Bucknell Women’s Basketball – you’re sure to hear something new on WVBU!

You can hear us on the air at 90.5 FM in the greater-Lewisburg area, and around the world right here on wvbu.com! Take WVBU with you on the go with the TuneIn app, available for iPhone and Android.

We welcome participation from all students and faculty. Visit our Join page to learn about the opportunity that awaits you at WVBU!
