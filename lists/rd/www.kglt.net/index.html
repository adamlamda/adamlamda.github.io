Blackfoot River Brewing Pint Night Sept. 17

Tip a Pint for KGLT at Blackfoot River Brewery Monday, September 17th from 5-8pm!

$1 from every pint sold will benefit KGLT - Southwest Montana’s Alternative Public Radio Station.

This is a great chance for KGLT DJs and Helena listeners to get together, enjoy locally brewed beer and celebrate a new, more powerful signal in Helena at 89.1FM. Blackfoot River Brewing is located at 66 South Park Ave in Helena. See you there!

KGLT New Frequency in Helena

Thanks to the collaborative efforts of Last Chance Public Radio Association, Yellowstone Public Radio and KGLT, we are now broadcasting at 89.1 in Helena. This will result in a more consistent, stronger signal for KGLT! Yellowstone Public Radio will move from 89.1FM to its new signal at 88.5FM.
