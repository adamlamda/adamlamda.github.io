Radio Kansas Summer '18 Schedule

As the summer completes we’ve enjoyed the restoration of live news to our midday schedule. For most of the time before I took over as manager in 2009, Radio Kansas offered morning classics, news at noon and classics ‘til ATC. This schedule resulted in our most successful era of service! During this period our Midday, news program was as long as 90 minutes. We think there’s a greater logic to a single hour at noon, but NPR didn’t offer us an option for a single hour until this spring!

As with any schedule change, this is undertaken for the sake of superior service to the public.    read more...
