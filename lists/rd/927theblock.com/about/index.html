About Us

Source: Other / other

We have merged the power of the Internet and Radio to bring to you, our loyal listeners, new and fun ways to interact with 92.7 The Block, our on-air personalities, advertisers, news and more.

Take the time to surf our site and see what’s going on today in and around 92.7 the Block. We keep the content fresh for you – with updates every hour, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week – to provide you something new every time you visit.
