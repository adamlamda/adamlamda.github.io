G-DUB Internet Radio Golden West College

 

The  Internet Radio and TV club are a  Digital Media/Arts Department Club.

The goal of the club is to:

  • Serve as a hands-on lab and training facility for students who are interested in pursuing careers in internet radio, television and digital media

The club is open to students enrolled in Digital Media/Arts classes at Golden West and to students who are interested in learning more about careers in digital media, digital arts internet radio and television.
