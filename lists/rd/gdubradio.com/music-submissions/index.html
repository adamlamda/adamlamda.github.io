Music Submissions

Are you an artist, band, producer, or label director and want to send us your music for airplay?

To submit music to G-DUB Radio, you MUST follow the guidelines below for consideration

Please send a copy of your CD or record to the following address:

Golden West College

G-DUB Internet Radio

Attention: Music Director

15744 Golden West Street

Huntington Beach, Ca. 92647

Enclose CDs in standard jewel case/digipack packaging with a clear spine label. Avoid, if possible, sending CDs in sleeves as they can (and do) get lost in the shuffle.

A copy of your release and a thorough one-sheet about you and the music is plenty. Extras such as lengthy press clippings or unwieldy folders make organization difficult and typically delay review of your material.

G-DUB Radio will not play music with obscenities or indecent content. All submitted music must be edited in accordance with FCC standards.
