The Rickey Smiley Morning Show

Rickey Smiley has taken the world by storm with his unique blend of comedy and originality. The Rickey Smiley Morning Show is a mix of laughter, community outreach, social political awareness and celebrity news.

Funnyman Rickey Smiley is known for making millions laugh for over 20 years. Smiley has earned a reputation for delivering for audiences on and off stage. With the distinct ability to take everyday observations and turn them into comedic gold, Smiley is a standout in the entertainment world and one of the few "clean" comics amongst the upper echelon of contemporary great comedians.

Rickey Smiley is the only comedian who has had the honor of hosting BET's Comic View for two seasons. Over the years, he has made numerous television and movie appearances including "The Rickey Smiley Show" - a sitcom, starring Rickey as a comedian, nationally syndicated radio personality, and single father of three; a character a loosely based on his own life. The show's stories are derived from Rickey's attempt to raise his children by himself and still have a personal life.

Rickey Smiley is a graduate of Alabama State University and proud member of Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Incorporated. His free time is spent with his loving children.

Wake up and go to work with Rickey every weekday. Listen from 5am - 9am only on The Blaze.

Dewayne Blaq

I was born and raised in Dallas Tx, where I developed a love for music at a very young age.

I used to always pretend that I had my own radio talk show as a kid. I would use my G.I Joe action figures as my audience. I started my radio career working for ABC Radio Group in the DFW area.

I moved to Tyler in October of 2003 and began at The Blaze in June of 2004.

I am a big Dallas Cowboy fan as well as a fan of the Mavericks. I'm a fan of good music, If it's hot, and the people are feeling it, I'm with it. My goal is to become a premier personality in the radio community.
