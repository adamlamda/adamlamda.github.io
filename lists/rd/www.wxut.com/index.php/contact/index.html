Contact Us


WXUT operates out of the University of Toledo Student Union in SU 2515. WXUT 88.3 FM is a service of UT and can be heard from 8 pm to 8 am Monday through Friday and all day on the weekend. We share our station with the Toledo Public Schools in the surrounding area.  WXUT is a Licensed Class A Non-Commercial FM Station operating a 100 watt transmitter located on the roof of Parks Tower.

If you are interested in becoming a DJ, or have have any other questions about our station, Click Here.
