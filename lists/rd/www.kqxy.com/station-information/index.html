UNSIGNED ARTISTS/MUSICIANS

Today’s Best Music Q94 is a Top 40 station, so we mainly play “the hits.”  However, we do welcome unsigned artists to send their material to us if they want to.  There are no promises or guarantees that your song will be played though.  You may send your demos to:  Q94, Attn: Program Director, 755 South 11th Street, Suite 102, Beaumont, TX 77701.
