Hosting a Show!
To host, tell us about your idea for a program, including:
  • Title of the show
  • Format (e.g. talk, call-in, music, etc. Please be specific.)
  • Description of content
  • Length of show (5 mins, 1 hour, etc.)
  • Run frequency (e.g. daily, weekly, etc.)
  • Suggested air time/day of week (e.g. Thursdays at 8-11 pm):
  • On air host(s) and their radio experience
  • Any potential underwriters/sponsors for this show
  • Your name and contact info (phone or email address)
