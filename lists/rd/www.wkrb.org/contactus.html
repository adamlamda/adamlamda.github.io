CONTACT US

WKRB accepts and encourages digital submissions!

When emailing or mailing music, please be sure to include information about the artist, including genre(s), track information, possible suggested tracks and contact information. WKRB is an FCC licensed station and only airs clean tracks. Please include radio friendly tracks. ***Submission of music does not guarantee airplay***

We are currently accepting submissions for the following genres:

  • EDM (Electronic Dance Music, all subgenres accepted)
  • Rock (All subgenres accepted)
  • Hip-Hop
  • R&B
  • Public Service Announcements
  • **NO COMMERCIALS!!!**
