WNUR Contact Information
In-studio contact info:
WNUR takes requests (depending what they are)! You can contact our DJ’s in-studio by telephone or via the chat box in our pop-out player. To call in and make a request, obtain track information, or chat with our DJs, reach our studio at 1 (847) 866-WNUR [9687]. We’d love to hear from you!

Please send comments about the station to WNUR station management at gm@wnur.org (nicholasanderson2019@u.northwestern.edu) or send written comments to our Postal Mail address:

Attn: [genre of music] WNUR 89.3 FM
1877 Campus Drive
Evanston, IL 60208

Please send all physical releases (CDs, LPs, Cassettes, MP3s, MDs, DATs, or anything else) to the address above. Important: include “Attn: [genre of music]” to ensure it gets to the right person, otherwise it’s likely to get mixed up in our sea of daily mail. Digital releases are left to the discretion of individual (show-specific) music directors and producers; email addresses for those shows are given below. For label or artist inquiries please email the appropriate Program Producer or Director below.
