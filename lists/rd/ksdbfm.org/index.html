Mission Statement

KSDB-FM is a non-profit, student run radio station serving Kansas State University and the city of Manhattan. KSDB provides original and diverse programming that aims to educate and entertain its listeners. Students are provided the unique opportunity to learn professional broadcasting skills in an engaging, hands-on enviornment. KSDB will work to foster the unique perspectives and skills of all involved students. They will use these skills to positively affect the student body at Kansas State, the Manhattan community, and beyond.
