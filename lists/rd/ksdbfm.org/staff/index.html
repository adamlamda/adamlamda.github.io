Student Volunteer/Experience Opportunities:
While our paid staff positions may be limited, you can get experience at your availability in any of the following areas at KSDB.

  • Graphic design (concert posters, event graphics, station materials)
  • Writing (news writing, concert/album reviews, interviews, scripts)
  • Sales (underwriting with local Manhattan businesses)
  • Event planning (promotions, campus organization partnerships)
  • Photography (live events, studio photography, student portraits)

Interested in joining the KSDB crew? Shoot an email with your information and interest to manager@ksdbfm.org and learn more about why we love student-run college radio.

Mission Statement

KSDB-FM is a non-profit, student run radio station serving Kansas State University and the city of Manhattan. KSDB provides original and diverse programming that aims to educate and entertain its listeners. Students are provided the unique opportunity to learn professional broadcasting skills in an engaging, hands-on enviornment. KSDB will work to foster the unique perspectives and skills of all involved students. They will use these skills to positively affect the student body at Kansas State, the Manhattan community, and beyond.
