    WXCI is the home of alternative music, WestConn live sports coverage and hot talk topics, 24/7. We take requests during all live shows at 203-837-9924 (WXCI). To hear the station, tune to 91.7FM in the Danbury, CT area OR click the small WXCI logo below OR download the TuneIn Radio app. Listen. Then, tell us what you think.
