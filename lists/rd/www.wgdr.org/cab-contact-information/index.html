CAB Contact Information

Community Advisory Board
The CAB consists of WGDR listeners and community members. Current programmers do not serve on the CAB. They are appointed by the Station Director, usually according to where they live. They meet regularly to discuss and describe listener’s concerns and viewpoints, and provide advice to station management.
