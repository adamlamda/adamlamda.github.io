Sunday, May 20, 2018

 Programming notes

As our programming continues to develop, there are a couple of updates to our program schedule:

 

Afternoon host, Daniel, has joined our air staff. Daniel is 13. He will be on air several afternoons each week and will also be involved in some off air projects.  We are working around his school and other commitments to give him the chance to grow and develop.

 

We are incorporating "information updates" of local news highlights weekday mornings around the bottom of the hour. This includes local news ad weather. Full stories and links provided on our Facebook and/or Twitter social media pages as appropriate.

 

Morning host Paul Glimmer will continue to provide a full service morning show with music, weather, time checks, traffic, sports, and more.

Tuesday, March 27, 2018

 WDXD music collection continues to grow

I want to say "thanks" to frIends and supporters who have donated music to WDXD. We have music libraries from several radio stations, including the now-silent WJEP 1020, WCVC 1330, WSBP 1580 (now WTCL), the former WGWD 93.3, and WAAC 92.9. Additionally, a number of individuals have donated collections of music.

This is the kind of grassroots support I truly appreciate.

Wednesday, December 7, 2016

 Thank you for your support during our first year of FM broadcasting!

Our first year of broadcasting on FM has certainly had its share of challenges - antenna bays out of phase, then repaired, only to be knocked out of phase by Hurricane Hermine! We are housed in a small converted construction site trailer with no restroom. Good thing we are next to the house! Businesses have not been real quick to spend any money with us, so I am thankful for those individuals who have stepped up to the plate to support WDXD.

Here is our budgometer as of 12/7/2016:

Total construction expenses $29,065.10

Total income received: $16,394.91

Amount needed to break even: {$12,670.19}
