FAQ:

WANT TO SEND US YOUR RECORD FOR AIRPLAY?

We prefer physical demos CDs (vinyl is always lovely too). Don’t have anything on physical yet? You can email us a link to your music at md@wmxm.org. We chart with CMJ for Top 200, RPM, Jazz, Hip-Hop, and World, but appreciate music of all genres. (See Snail Mail Address below)

INTERESTED IN JOINING WMXM?

WMXM is a college station run by students, but we do love passionate community DJs who can commit to a regular show. Student or not, email Fatima Hassan, our General manager at gm at wmxm dot org with information about yourself.

WANT TO DO A STUDIO SESSION, PLAY A GIG, OR BE INTERVIEWED?

WMXM DJs have full control over their shows. If you’re interested in doing anything on or off air, you can email your information to booking at WMXM dot org and if we’re interested we’ll get back to you. We are not a needy music blog, if we’re interested in you we will be in touch.

HAVE QUESTION COMMENT OR COMPLAINT?

Please send all questions, comments, or complaints to our station general manager Fatima Hassan at gm at WMXM dot org or call our office line (847)-735-5220. We take pride in our programming, and strive to be a beneficial alternative station for Lake County.
