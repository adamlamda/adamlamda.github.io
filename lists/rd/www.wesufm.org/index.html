Back to Full Power!

August 24, 2018 wesugm Comments 0 Comment

  FEEL THE POWER! After broadcasting at reduced power, for nearly 2 months following a direct lightening strike to our antenna, WESU has resumed broadcasting at 6,000 Watts ERP as licensed by the FCC.  Many thanks to our engineering staff for their hard work, Wesleyan University for having our back, and our volunteer staff and listeners for their patience!  STAY TUNED!

WESU Antenna Problem

WESU Antenna Problem

June 29, 2018 wesugm

On June 28th, at the tail end of a successful spring pledge drive, WESU was dealt a major technical blow, temporarily taking us off the FM airwaves. Our transmitter is OK but there is a problem with our antenna that needs to be repaired. Upon first internal inspection it appears the antenna took a direct lightning hit. We are  back on air broadcasting from a temporary antenna operating at reduced power until we can rebuild or purchase a new antenna….
