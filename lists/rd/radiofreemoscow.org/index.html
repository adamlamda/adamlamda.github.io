Be a KRFP Community Deejay!

The Summer season runs  Labor Day to MLK Day in January.  We train community members as deejays and radio hosts!  No radio experience is necessary.

Submit KRFPDeejayApplicationFall2018 (2 pages), along with Schedule Grid (1 page). Print all 3 pages, fill in, and bring or mail to the station.

For best results get you application in by Thurs., Aug 30th at 7:00 pm

If you don’t have a printer, paper applications are available at KRFP (114 E. 3rd St.) enter via the alley, or on the shelf in the hall outside Mikey’s Gyros.
