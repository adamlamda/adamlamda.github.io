KBEK 95.5 – The Voice of East Central Minnesota

The purpose of KBEK 95.5 FM is to unite, grow and strengthen the organizations, educators, government leaders, arts, commerce, listeners and others who define East Central Minnesota’s character through its live and local programming, thereby creating a sense of community.
