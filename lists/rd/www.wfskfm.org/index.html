What's the 411? with Sharon Kay

Join host & executive producer Sharon Kay for What's the 411?  The show, on the air since 2001, is an empowerment /
encouragement talk program which is not only informative, but uplifting.  It's heard live on WFSK Wednesdays during drive time from 4:00pm to 6:00pm and Sunday mornings from 7:00am to 9:00am.   

      Sharon is an award winning veteran news reporter, anchor, talk show host and longtime community empowerment advocate.
Every Wednesday evening at 6pm and Sunday at 2pm (cst), multiple Grammy Award winning musician Christian McBride hosts  Jazz Night in America.

BLUESTIME IN THE CITY’ is a top-rated program that features the very best of the blues genre’s artists and music. Fast-paced and upbeat, BTIC is an experience that thrills the audience every weekend with traditional, soul, party and contemporary blues.

Hosted by multi-Emmy award winning Television Producer Rojene Bailey, the blues are presented in a style that smoothly blends entertainment and the latest news from the world of the blues.

Who's in the Gospel Top 20? 

Grammy Award winner Dr. Bobby Jones hosts today's hottest weekly gospel countdown show. The Bobby Jones Gospel Countdown is two hours of the week's fully researched top twenty gospel tracks.

   Get your gospel praise on Sunday mornings on JAZZY 88   5am -  7am by tuning into the Bobby Jones Gospel Countdown.

    Each weekend, Bobby picks the top Gospel Hits and Interviews some of the Top Stars in Gospel Music. These weekend programs showcase America’s Most Beloved Gospel music performers. Bobby Jones Gospel, for which he is best known, has been seen on Black Entertainment Television (BET) since November 1980 and is one of the network’s most popular shows.
