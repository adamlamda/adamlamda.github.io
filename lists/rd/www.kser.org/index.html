News

  • Democracy Now! - Mon 5am, 7am & 5pm

    Democracy Now! is a daily progressive, nonprofit, independently syndicated news hour that airs on more than 1,250 radio, television, satellite and cable TV networks around the globe. Amy Goodman and Juan Gonzalez host. Heard at 5am, 7am and 5pm.

  • Flashpoints - Mon 6am

    Flashpoints is an award-winning daily investigative newsmagazine broadcast on the Pacifica Radio network, hosted by Dennis Bernstein. Through original reports and some of the key investigative reporters of our time, Flashpoints goes to the core of stories in the areas of government and corporate criminality, human rights, and economic justice.

  • Thom Hartmann Show - Mon 8am & 3pm

    Like Democracy Now, this program looks at the news and issues of the day from a progressive perspective. The show is all about returning to a people centered democracy. It’s a 1-hour recap of Thom Hartmann’s best guests of the day.  

  • Sound Living - Mon 4pm

    With a focus on North Puget Sound, Sound Living brings you local commentary, national perspectives, Snohomish and Island county events, people and stories into your car and radio. From environmental issues to political discussions to interesting ideas and people, Sound Living is a look at the places we call home.

    Listen to past programs...

  • Focus On Aging - Mon 6pm

    Joan Bethel talks with Jane Duran, Chair of the Snohomish County “Out of Darkness” walk to prevent suicide and Justine McCover, a local suicide prevention activist.  They’ll talk about suicide, suicide prevention and the September 29th “Out of Darkness” walk to support the work of the American Foundation for Suicide Prevention.

Music

 

  • Caribbean Sounds - Mon 7pm

    Noel Williams guides you through the Caribbean's rich musical traditions. You'll hear Soca, Calypso, Garifina, and all your favorites from the islands and the coasts. Born and raised in Belize, Noel brings you his personal take on the sounds and history of music from across the southern sea.

  • Blue Moon Lounge - Mon 8:30pm

    David Ballew serves up an eclectic mix of Americana, twang, R&B, surf, girl groups, power pop and a weekly dose of old time gospel. If you have a taste for old and new, happy and blue, tune in Monday nights from 8:30 to 10:30pm.

  • Celtic Caravan - Mon 10:30pm

    Climb aboard the Celtic Caravan as we explore multiple genres with a celtic flavor deep into the night. Is ait an mac an saol.
