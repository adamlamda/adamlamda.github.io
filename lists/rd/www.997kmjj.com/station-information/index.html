1 Hosted an Open House On Thursday, October 26th 2017 from 5p-7p, Cumulus partnered with the Bossier Chamber of Commerce to host an open house at our broadcasting studios in Bossier City, LA. Attendees were introduced to SEU’s, staff members, with Vice President, Business Manager, Promotions Director, and account executives present. Resumes and applications were collected. All the SEU stations promoted the event.
2 Participate in a Career Fair On March 16th, 2017 SEU’s participated in a career fair at Louisiana College in Pineville, LA. Our Promotions Director and members of our street team participated in the event, manned a booth, and spoke with attendees about career opportunities, job openings, and internships. Applications offered. Station vehicle and booth/tents were assembled on location.
3 Participate in a Career Expo On December 6, 2017 SEU’s participated in a spring registration and internship/career expo event at Southern University in Shreveport, LA. KMJJ station on was on site with street team members, and booth set-up. SEU’s Promotions Director, with full-time KMJJ (station) on-air station staff participating in the event. Applications were accepted, and internship opportunities were discussed, along with part-time employment opportunities.
4 Participated in a Job and Internship Fair Cumulus SEU’s participated in a Spring Job and Internship Fair at LSU-Shreveport on March 22nd, 2017. SEU Promotions Director attended with booth set-up. Current students and alumni were able to speak with station representatives about part-time, internships, and potential full-time roles
5 Participated in a Career Fair On May 11th, 2017 SEU’s participated in a Louisiana Workforce Commission Career Fair at 104 Market Street in Shreveport, LA. SEU’s Business Manager and Promotions Director on location with booth to discuss employment opportunities and career path growth
