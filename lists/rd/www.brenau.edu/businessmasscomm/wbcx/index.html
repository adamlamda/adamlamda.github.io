89.1 WBCX Gainesville – The Voice of Brenau!

Listen Live

89.1 WBCX Gainesville, GA, The Voice Of Brenau, was created in 1977 and is owned and operated by Brenau University. WBCX is the only FM music station licensed to and located in Gainesville, and Brenau students and community volunteers have run this great radio station for 40 years.

Call the station at +1-770-538-4744, or for more information please contact Director of BroadcastingJay Andrews at +1-770-538-4709.
