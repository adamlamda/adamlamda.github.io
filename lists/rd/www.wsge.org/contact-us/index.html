How to obtain copies of Annual Financial Reports Filed with CPB

WSGE files an Annual Financial Report with the Corporation for Public Broadcasting every year by February 28 for the previous fiscal year, as required by CPB. This Annual Financial Report is attested to by WSGE’s independent auditor as to its accuracy and directly corresponds to the organization’s audited financial statements.

To obtain copies of this report please contact us during regular business hours Monday through Friday. Requests may be made by telephone at (704) 922-6552, email to Cathis Hall, or mail to: WSGE, Attn: Station Manager, 201 Highway 321 South, Dallas, NC 28034.
