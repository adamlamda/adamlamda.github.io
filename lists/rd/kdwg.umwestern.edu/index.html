90.9 KDWG Campus Radio provides alternative and educational programming to the campus of the University of Montana Western and the surrounding Beaverhead Valley.  KDWG is a free-form FM station that allows our students, faculty, and community member volunteer DJ’s play an eclectic mix of music. See below for the DJ information, and contact our General Manager for DJing opportunities.

If you have an upcoming event, class project, or local issue you would like KDWG to address, please contact our Program Director and fill out the PSA Request Form giving a minimum of 5 business days notice.
