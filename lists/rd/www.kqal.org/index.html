Welcome to KQAL.org

We’re glad you found us. KQAL is located on the campus of Winona State University in Winona, Minnesota. Our programming is as unique as the students who operate KQAL on a daily basis.From arts and cultural programs to a variety of music formats, plus news & sports, KQAL has something for every listener. Listen live by clicking on the microphone, or tune in to 89.5 FM!

We welcome and appreciate your Support of KQAL as well. Thank You!

 The KQAL App is essential, you can get it  in the App Store or Google Play.

Have a request? You can call the KQAL studio line: 507.457.2222
