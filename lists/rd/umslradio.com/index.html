SEMPA

STUDENT ELECTRONIC MEDIA PROFESSIONALS ASSOCIATION

The Student Electronic Media Professionals’ Association oversees The U-UMSL Radio Station, a student-run radio station that strives to offer unique programming while providing a service that is both educational and entertaining to the University of Missouri-Saint Louis community and “The U TV”, a student-run video team that strives to offer pre- and post-production opportunities and provide informative and fun videos for the campus and the community. We also offer practicum opportunities for Media Studies and Communications Majors.
