Station Information

 

Rock 100.5 the Legendary KATT radio station is located in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma in the United States.  The station signed on full-time in September of 1976 even though some broadcasts date back to 1975 under the call letters of KJAK.  The format of the KATT at the time was “album oriented rock”.  Today it is considered “Active Rock”.  Start your day each morning at 6am with Rick Walker & Brad Copeland with Lauren Craun and Ryan Day as supporting cast members.  Rock out til you clock out with Greg Zoobeck at 10am.  Jake Daniels gets you home with the Afternoon Traffic Jam from 3p to 7pm.  Allison Chainz puts you to bed at night!  And Cris Anger pulls the overnight ritual. The KATT also features “Hard Drive” and the “Local Talent Show with Jay Ramone” as special programming shows.  There are many ways to get in touch with us.  The request lines are always the first option.  They are 405-460-5288 and 888-THE-KATT.  We are also on Facebook and Twitter. We hope to rock your world everyday!!
