Welcome to WPUB Radio

Birthed in 1974, WPUB is Pace University’s one and only student run radio station. We can be heard all over the world 24 hours a day 7 days a week. WPUB prides itself on fostering a creative environment that is inclusive of all students at Pace University. We specialize in providing a strong variety of music and talk based shows with absolutely no commercials. We are also responsible for hosting engaging and relevant events catered to the Pace Community.
