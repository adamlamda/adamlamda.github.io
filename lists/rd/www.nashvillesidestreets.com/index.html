Ronnie Dean’s Music. LISTEN!! 




It’s 2017, and Nashville Side Streets’ 4th year in production with live video streaming worldwide.
We upgraded our computers this year so we can now broadcast live on 3 Facebook pages and our own Watch Live Page simultaneously, as well as broadcasting live on YouTube, Google and Twitter.
Remember that our “Watch Live Page” broadcast can be seen in full HD because we are running on fiber.
It’s a lot of fun getting all this new unsigned talent on our show because you get to see and hear what the artists and songwriters are really going through as they live this life of music.
I figured out myself, a long time ago, that if you are born to do this music thing, you will, no matter what it takes.

Remember-if you want to audition for the show, go to the top of the page and hit the Contact tab, and follow the directions.
Watch our Schedule Page and come check out our new talent this year as we book some of the best unsigned acts from around the world that you’ll ever hear or see.
Check out our archives tab for some great talent that has floated through our studio.
Email us and keep an eye out for the new Nashville Side Streets van as we travel through the US to find more “exceptional hidden talent”.

2017 is shaping up to be a great year!!
Thanks for all your support and for watching Nashville Side Streets!

Ronnie Dean, producer and Nancy Hanson, co-producer
