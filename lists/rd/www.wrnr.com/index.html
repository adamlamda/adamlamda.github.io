#PressOnAnnapolis

 

Carrie talks with Aaron Yealdhall aka Annapolis based musician SKRIBE - about his Press On Annapolis movement - dedicated to the lives lost in the tragedy at The Capital in 2018. The publication's resilience inspired us to spread this 'Freedom Of Press' spirit on a worldwide scale. If you would like to support the movement, you can order shirts and stickers here: pressonannapolis.myshopify.com/
All proceeds benefit The Capital Gazette Families Fund
