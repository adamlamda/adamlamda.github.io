Welcome to WhiskeyBoy Radio

Welcome to WhiskeyBoy Radio! WBR – the show that started it all!! We feature the best in local Rock and national unsigned and new artists. Along with our regular programing of great new music we also feature talk shows that you can download as a podcast. If you’re looking for laughs, great music, controversial topics, and moments that are guaranteed to make to ask ‘What is Wrong with them?’, then you’ve found it!!
Do you like great music and humor? WhiskeyBoy Radio is the place to get all that and more. We are a podcast with an outrageous cast, edgy topics, and we feature the best in new unsigned artists! We have regular comedy bits, prank calls, local music , bizarre news from around the globe and solutions to our listeners problems! We also feature live video shows and have other shows coming soon.

Another Damn Talk Show... Live!

Join us every week where Chrys Starr and Bijan Ranjbar bring you entertaining commentary on pretty much any topic that comes to mind.
Another Damn Talk show is hosted by Chrys Starr, Bijan Ranjar, and Anita... veterans of online broadcasting.  Every week they bring you a unique and fresh approach to current topics. ADTS features culture, entertainment, and other news, the "Question of the Week", the Drink of the Show, and much much more!  ADTS is also the host show of the yearly 'Concert for the Cause'; a breast cancer benefit hosted at the Hard Rock Cafe in Pittsburgh, PA at the end of September each year, to kick off Breast Cancer Awareness Month in October.
