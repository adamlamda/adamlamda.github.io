Station Manager: Meredith Mattlin
Office Hours: 3-4pm Friday
Hi everyone! I’m Meredith, and I’m studying Medicine, Health, & Society and Film. I serve as WRVU’s Station Manager! This role means I oversee all the operations of the station: I direct an awesome executive staff, help our Program Director with the blog, coordinate/conduct interviews, film in-studio sessions, help develop the radio station’s ethos alongside our many talented DJs, and make this place a resource and community for music lovers in Nashville. I also have a show called “Little Pharma” on Sundays at 5pm CST whose obnoxious name is loosely based on a residual pseudo-angst against the wrath of real-life Big Pharma (and a very real reliance on its whim)–as well as a reflection of my interest in studying the medical world, which I do in my academic life. God, sorry, I need to chill, right? That’s what this show’s about. Or not. Who knows. We’re all just figuring stuff out. I play a lot of angry melancholy post-punk stuff (basically post-anything; I hear millennials love that) and sadboy jams even though I hate the term “sadboy.” If you ever want to hang or read me cryptic messages or complain about life’s troubles, feel free to stop by my office hours.

Or, contact me at wrvugm@gmail.com
