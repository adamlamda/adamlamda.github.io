Music Submission

Posted By: subodh December 4, 2014 @ 6:41 am Uncategorized

Power 92 accepts new music submissions via CD ONLY. All material music must be copywritten prior to submission. Music must be “radio quality”, professionally produced and mastered in a professional studio. All music must be properly “edited” for broadcast. Profanity, violence and sexual content must be properly censored in order for airplay consideration.

CDs can be sent to the following address:
Power 92
6336 Calumet Avenue
Hammond, Indiana 46324
ATTN : Music Director

Or you can email music to: power92music@crawfordbroadcasting.com

No music submitted to Power 92 for consideration may be returned.
