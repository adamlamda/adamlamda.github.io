Become DJ

Any PSU student can have a show on KPSU! We also welcome community members to participate at KPSU and they are required to pay a per term fee of $75 or $50 for alumni. 

 

Our training process is relatively simple and consists of five steps; two informational sessions, an open note test, and two hands on sessions conducted in the broadcast booth. We have training sessions during Fall, Winter and Spring Term. We do not conduct training sessions during the summer.

 

If you are interested in becoming a DJ, email our Volunteer Director at [email protected] to be notified of upcoming training sessions.
