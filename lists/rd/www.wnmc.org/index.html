Our listener promise

Greetings Internet listeners! The listen player to the left may not work for the next few days. Try this page: LISTEN HERE » instead. Sorry for any inconvenience.

WNMC's mission is to provide educational and culturally enriching programming to its northern Michigan service area. We also actively seek to promote and support the educational and cultural missions of NMC and other local entities with similar missions. Learn more »

Our station broadcasts a variety of informational and musical programming, ranging from news and talk on Mornings (6–8:15 a.m. Monday–Friday) to flagship jazz-mix programming on weekdays (8:15 a.m.–5 p.m.) to alternative rock, African music, electronic and much more »

Current Playlist/Chat with the DJ: Some of our DJs are available for online chat through our Spinitron playlists during their shows. Of course, that availability is limited — they've got other things to do! — but you can say howdy, sing their praises, make a request or whatever by viewing the current playlist here »

Find us on Twitter: @wnmc is our general twitter feed. @wnmcradio is our playlist feed (every song we play).

Ways to Listen: in addition to our radio signal in the Traverse City area, you can also listen online. If your data connection is shaky, you can even listen via your voice connection: just call Zeno Radio at (484) 718-0464 and you'll soon have WNMC on your phone. Your carrier's normal voice rates for U.S. calls apply.

Miss your favorite show? RadioFreeAmerica hosts the past two weeks of WNMC's programming so you never need to miss a show again. Can't stay up for In the Red on Friday night? Wake up on Saturday morning and listen then. And you can listen live as well! Listen now or later at WNMC on RadioFreeAmerica.
