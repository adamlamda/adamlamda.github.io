Pinkney Hall: August Themes

August 11, 2016 // Events, News

Every Friday morning, tune into Pinkney Hall. During August, you will hear music with the following themes, along with newly released music and music from artists appearing in Pittsburgh. All ​in​ the space of one hour – 9 AM to 10 AM.

August 5 – Car crashes!

August 12 – Kenny Joe will be on vacation, so you will have a surprise guest DJ or automation.

August 19 – Still on vacation.

August 26 – We’ll celebrate two August birthday boys – Elvis Costello and Van Morrison.

Pinkney Hall: June Themes

June 9, 2016 // Events, News

We are sorry that technical problems kept us off the air on the last two Fridays in May. For the first three Fridays in June, we will honor three folks who had a lot of influence on American music, Bob Dyan, Harry Smith and Sam Phillips. You will also will hear newly released music and music from artists scheduled to appear in Pittsburgh. So, tune in Friday mornings from 9 AM to 10 AM and check it out.

June 3– We’ll be honoring Bob Dylan.

June 10 – We’ll be honoring Harry Smith.

June 17 – We’ll be honoring Sam Phillips.

June 24 – Monkeys!! (And we do not mean The Monkees!)

If you have any requests, please call 412-268-9728 during the show.
