Capital:

The official capital of Bolivia - Sucre.

Location:

State in the central part of South America. In the north and east by Brazil, south-east - with Paraguay in the south - to Argentina, in the west - with Chile, in the north-west - with Peru.

Climate:

Tropical and sub-equatorial plains, sharply-continental in the mountains .. Mean temperature in summer (December-February) vary from +21-24 C over the plains areas to +3 C on slopes of the Cordillera. In winter (May-August) average temperature ranges from +19 to -1 C, respectively.
Population:
Population about 8,3 million people. About 25% of the population are Bolivians. About 15% of the population are descendants of Spanish settlers and 2% - of African descent. The rest of the inhabitants were Indian tribes Quechua (25-30%), Aymara (17-30%) and others.

Language:

Official language - Spanish, the majority of Bolivians also speaks the languages of the Aymara, Quechua and Guarani.

Cuisine:

By national dishes include empanadas Celta Vigo (often served with garlic, raisins, potatoes, hot sauce and pepper baked in dough), or with potatoes - Tucuman, fried steak and eggs, rice and fried banana "lomo-mentado" , roast chicken with potatoes and salad "pikante de polo" or simply roasted over an open fire chicken "polos-spedo", "polos-broster or polos la Brasa" soup "chayro, dried and roasted meat alpaca cup of "dried potatoes in a special way" Chuco "(original chips), thick soups, lakuas, fried potatoes, onions, tomatoes and pertsemi sausage, mashed bananas or yucca meat alpaca Masako" beef in all forms .

Attractions:

National Parks Isiboro-Secure, Uanchaka, Pilon Lajas, Madidi, Toro, Toro, Ambor, Sajama, Noel Kempff Mercado, Kotapata, Carrasco, Kaa-Lya del Gran Chaco National Reserve Manuripi-Heath, Biosphere Reserve del Beni, reserves Eve-Eve, Ros-Blanco and Negro Forest Reserve Rio Grande Masikure, Preserve Cape Huang, preserve flora and fauna Tarikua, Biosphere Reserve Cordillera Sama, the Andean Fauna Reserve Eduardo-Avaroa, National Reserves fauna Ulla-Ulla and Andina Eduardo Avaroa and arboretum Itunez

Currency:

Bs equal to 100 centavos. U.S. Dollars accepted almost everywhere.

Nature:

A characteristic feature of local nature reserves and parks is an amazing diversity of flora and fauna, and scenic landscapes of their member territories.

Religion:

Mainly Christian Catholics and Protestants.

Conditions of entry:

To obtain a visa in advance to submit to the Consular Section of the Embassy of Bolivia valid passport (must be valid at least 6 months after the trip), a copy of the 1-st page of the passport photograph, the original round-trip tickets, a copy of the ticket the whole route, a completed application form with 1 photography. Consular fee - U.S. $ 30. In the case of visits to relatives or friends must be invited (fax). Tourist visa is valid for entry within a month after issuance.

Medical features:

Medical insurance of the international sample is strongly recommended.

Resorts:

At an altitude of 5486 meters, 55 km from La Paz, is the highest ski resort in the world - Chakaltayya. Sucre - Bolivia's official capital and one of the highland cities of the world. City of Potosi lies 164 km from Sucre on a barren, windswept plains. La Paz - the political and commercial center of Bolivia, seat of government agencies and most mountainous capital of the world. Lake Titicaca - the highest lake in the world.
